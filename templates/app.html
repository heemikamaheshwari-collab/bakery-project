How to install on Android

  1. Tap "Download for Android" above. A file called shrutis_cakesanddmore.apk will start downloading.
  2. Open the file. Tap the notification when it finishes, or find it in your Downloads folder.
  3. Allow installs from this source. Android shows a one-time prompt — tap "Settings" → toggle "Allow from this source" → come back. You only need to do this once.
  4. Tap Install. If Play Protect warns about an "unrecognized app", tap "Install anyway". The icon will appear on your home screen.

Why the warnings? This app isn't on the Play Store yet — Android always shows extra prompts for direct downloads. The app is the same site you're already on, packaged for your phone.
